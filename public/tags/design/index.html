New ZImpact bar

13/Nov 2013 By Jean-Christophe Lavocat 1 min. read

Features

Initially, the display of your score has been made in a bubble like tooltip on top of your profile picture. But today, things have changed. The new ZImpact bar is out ! We are glad to roll out this new graphical bar that shows your level on a rainbow color scale bar. It’s a great occasion for you to share your profile and get a higher score. If your score is 12, it means that you have at least 12 articles with 12 shares on every social networks.
